My name is László Fazekas, a computer system engineer, applied mathematician, and compulsive writer.

I typically pen articles on topics such as blockchain, artificial intelligence, and brain-computer interfaces, but I also have a few hard sci-fi short stories to my name.

Just do it! Do something cool today. This is my philosophy of life...
